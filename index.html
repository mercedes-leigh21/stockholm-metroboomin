The Tunnelbana opened in 1950 right as Stockholm city hit its all-time peak (808 k in 1960). Over the next 20 years the city shrank as the metro enabled the ABC-städer suburbs (Vällingby, Farsta, Skärholmen). The city core only recovered after 2000.
